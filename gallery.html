INSPIRO-2020

INSPIRO-2020 was the annual flagship event of the IEEE ADIT Student Branch organised on October 9th, 10th and 11th 2020 in the virtual mode due to the Covid-19 pandemic. During this event, 13 eminent speakers from around the globe delivered talks with an aim to create awareness amongst students with focus areas like career development, women empowerment, leadership in organizations, personal branding, entrepreneurship, engineering, professional journey with IEEE and practice of innovation.

INSPIRO 2.0

INSPIRO 2.0, a self-organised event by IEEE ADIT Student Branch, was held on the 13th & 14th of September 2019 at ADIT College Campus, New Vallabh-Vidyanagar. The event was attended by over 400 participants and 10 prominent personalities from all over the country who delivered their powerful and influential speeches on diverse topics of - technical advancements, personal development, entrepreneurship, leadership, literature, entertainment and awareness on healthcare and social welfare.

INSPIRO-2018

INSPIRO-2018, an event brought to success by the IEEE ADIT Student Branch, was conducted on 28th and 29th September 2018, at the ADIT College Campus, New Vallabh Vidyanagar. The event was enriched with the presence of 12 keynote speakers where they provided talks focusing on various significant areas like entrepreneurship, personal development, business and start-up, women empowerment, student development, rural development, healthcare and the upcoming journey with IEEE.
